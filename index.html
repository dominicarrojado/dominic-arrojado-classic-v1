About me

A couple of things that you need to know.

Hi there, I’m Dominic Arrojado. I'm a humble developer who is passionate about what I do. I'm currently living in Quezon City, Philippines. During my college days, I have developed a pizza delivery website, a group chat, and a school enrollment system. That's when I thought I really like building websites and web applications. I graduated with Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology at Treston International College in 2015. After that, I took a month rest from life.

After my vacation, I needed to return back to life and get a job. After a few applications, I got a job at Hashtag Interactive as a Web Developer and after a year I was promoted as a Full Stack Developer. And the rest is history...
